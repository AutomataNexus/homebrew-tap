## NexusFoundry Commercial License

### Free Use

NexusFoundry binaries may be used at no cost for:
- Personal projects and experimentation
- Academic research and education
- Evaluation and proof-of-concept development
- Open-source projects

### Commercial License Required

A commercial license is required for:
- Production deployment in commercial products or services
- Integration into proprietary build pipelines
- Compilation of models for commercial edge devices
- Use by organizations with annual revenue exceeding $1M USD

### Commercial License Includes

- Unlimited model compilations

- On-premise deployment assistance (Enterprise tier)
